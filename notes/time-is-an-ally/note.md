## Time-is-an-Ally V3

Time-is-an-Ally V3 is not a forecasting formula. It models value not as a fixed line, but as a non-decreasing conceptual trajectory whose slope updates over time as Market and Influence accumulate under external signals.

- V2 established the separation between time amplification and discoverability.
- V3 specifies the structural form of amplified value as a trajectory rather than a fixed line.
- Collapse resets the market regime, not the underlying accumulated value trajectory.
