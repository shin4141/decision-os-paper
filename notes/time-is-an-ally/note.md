# Time-is-an-Ally V3

Time-is-an-Ally V3 is not a forecasting formula.

V2 established that Time-is-an-Ally is not a forecasting formula and separated time amplification from discoverability under an external-signal re-evaluation gate.

V3 specifies the structural form left open in V2: value is modeled not as a fixed line, but as a non-decreasing conceptual trajectory whose slope updates over time as Market and Influence accumulate under external signals.

It is intended to detect changes in slope rather than waves in price, especially in systems whose Genesis compounds over time.

- V2 established the separation between time amplification and discoverability.
- V3 specifies the structural form of amplified value as a trajectory rather than a fixed line.
- Collapse resets the market regime, not the underlying accumulated value trajectory.
