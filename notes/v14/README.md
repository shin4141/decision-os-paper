# Decision-OS V14 Research Note

## Resource Justice at the Joint Layer

This folder contains the initial V14 research note on Resource Justice, focusing on joint-layer failures in long-horizon AI operation.

Core claim:

> Resource Justice failures often occur not at the content layer, but at the joint layer.

V14 introduces the idea that AI systems can fail by returning unresolved burden, responsibility, temporal confusion, idea-maturation loss, and protected-object misidentification to the human through weak joints.
