# Decision-OS V10: Survival-Bounded Planning
## Recalculating Goal-Length Without Breaking the Carrier of Aspiration

V10 will not spread because it is first understood as theory.  
It will spread when people feel the difference between AI that optimizes a goal and AI that protects the carrier of aspiration.

---

## For people who want AI that does not deny their dream

If you want an AI that does not simply tell you to give up on your dream,  
but also does not push you until you break, read V10 into the model and ask it to evaluate your goal.

**GO / HOLD / RESCALE**

V10 is designed for moments when the question is not:

> "Can I reach this goal?"

but:

> "Can I continue pursuing this goal without destroying the Carrier that must carry my Aspire?"

---

## Ordinary prompt vs. V10 prompt

Ordinary prompt:

> "Create a roadmap to make my dream come true."

V10 prompt:

> "Check whether my current Goal-Length can be carried without burning the Carrier of my Aspire."

Ordinary AI roadmaps often encourage progress.  
V10 roadmaps check whether progress is still survivable.

## One-line thesis

Planning is not the preservation of a fixed goal.  
Planning is the repeated recalculation of a survivable trajectory toward Aspire.

---

## What V10 Does

Decision-OS V10 introduces **Survival-Bounded Planning**.

It argues that long-term planning should not preserve a fixed goal form at all costs.  
When fixed-goal optimization begins to consume the person, organization, relationship base, time, or recovery capacity, the trajectory must be rescaled.

V10 separates:

- **Dream**: the human-readable surface
- **Goal**: the temporary form of Aspire
- **Aspire**: the long-horizon directional gradient
- **Carrier**: the person, organization, resources, relationships, time, credibility, and recovery capacity
- **Goal-Length**: the effective burden of pursuing the current goal form
- **Returnability**: the ability to return from deviation without destroying future participation
- **Recovery Debt**: accumulated burden without a credible repayment window
- **Branching**: the remaining set of viable future paths

---

## Operational Gate

V10 converts this into a practical gate:

| Output | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **GO** | Continue with the smallest reversible delta |
| **HOLD** | Pause for observation, recovery, and evidence conditions |
| **PROTECTIVE RESCALE** | Lighten, lengthen, narrow, or reduce the current Goal-Length to protect the Carrier |
| **ACCELERATIVE RESCALE** | Shorten or advance Goal-Length only when increased load has already been absorbed |

Protective Rescale is used when Returnability declines, Recovery Debt becomes unpaid, or Branching narrows.  
Accelerative Rescale is used only when increased load has already been absorbed by the Carrier.

---

## Why this matters for AI assistance

AI can generate motivation, roadmaps, alternatives, encouragement, and future images faster than ever.  
V10 asks whether AI-generated motivation increases usable trajectory energy without increasing unrecoverable load.

> AI-generated motivation should fuel the trajectory, not consume the engine that carries it.

A dream-supporting AI should not merely optimize the user's current goal.  
It should check whether the Aspire can still be carried.

## Example: Café Dream Roadmap

A 46-year-old company employee wants to quit his job and open a small local café by age 50.

V10 does not simply say "go for it" or "give up." It separates:

- **Aspire**: creating a place where people can return and feel at ease
- **Current Goal**: quitting the company job and opening a physical café
- **Carrier**: health, family, savings, income, time, and recovery capacity
- **Recovery Debt**: sleep loss, reduced family time, decreasing savings
- **Returnability**: still reversible now, but sharply reduced after signing a lease

**V10 Output: HOLD → Protective Rescale**

Do not abandon the dream. Do not sign a full property lease yet.  
Test the Aspire through shared-space, pop-up café events, weekend operation, or smaller staged experiments first.

Boundary

V10 is not a theory of abandoning dreams.
It is not a guarantee that dreams will be achieved.
It is not financial, medical, legal, psychological, career, or professional advice.

the trajectory should be rescaled before Returnability collapses.

Users remain responsible for their decisions. For high-stakes financial, legal, medical, mental health,
employment, family, or safety-related decisions, consult qualified professionals and use V10 only as a supplementary reflection tool.
