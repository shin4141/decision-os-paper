# Decision-OS V7 Addendum V2: Why Aspire, Why PIC

This addendum clarifies two core questions in Decision-OS V7:

1. Why **Aspire** is necessary as a directional condition for self-recursive intelligence  
2. Why **PIC** (Phase-Invariant Core) is necessary as the structural condition that prevents order-dependent collapse

## What this addendum does

This document is not a full restatement of V7.  
It is a focused clarification note that explains why V7 requires both:

- **Aspire** — to preserve direction, intention, and long-horizon orientation
- **PIC** — to preserve structural consistency under recursive updates and different update orders

In short:

- Without **Aspire**, recursion can become directionless
- Without **PIC**, recursion can become unstable or order-dependent

## Why this matters

Decision-OS V7 defines intelligence not only by capability, but by whether a system can:

- keep a direction,
- update recursively,
- and remain structurally coherent through those updates

This addendum isolates that logic and makes the necessity of Aspire and PIC explicit.
